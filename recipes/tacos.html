Birria Tacos

Description

Birria tacos are typically made with slow-cooked meat, such as beef or lamb, marinated in the spicy birria sauce. You can use a variety of cuts of beef, including chuck roast, short ribs, or shoulder roast. 2. Tortillas: Birria tacos are traditionally served on small, soft corn tortillas.

Ingredients

  • Chilis
  • Chuck
  • Bones
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Broth
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Sear the meat
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Braise the meat
  4. Assemble the tacos
