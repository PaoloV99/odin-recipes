Schnitzel

Description

What is Schnitzel? Schnitzel: a thin slice of meat, usually tenderized by pounding, coated in flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs, and then pan-fried until perfectly crispy with a deep golden brown crispy breading that covers the tender, juicy meat.

Ingredients

  • breadcrumbs
  • flower
  • eggs
  • pork cutlet
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare flower, whisked eggs and breadcrumbs in three separate plates.
  2. Season the pork cutlet.
  3. Bread the pork cutlet.
  4. Get a pan of neutral oil to medium high heat.
  5. Cook steak in oil until the breading has a good colour.
