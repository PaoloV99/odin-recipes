Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Our best ever spaghetti bolognese is super easy and a true Italian classic with a meaty, chilli sauce. This pasta bolognese recipe is sure to become a family favourite.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Salt & pepper
  • Tomato paste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti while making sauce.
  2. Cook onions, garlic and herbs in oil.
  3. Add meat and brown.
  4. Add tomtato paste and more spices.
  5. Add diced tomatos.
  6. Add tomato sauce and put heat low.
